Features

Everything Your WooCommerce Store Needs

One plugin connects your store to a world of payment options — with full native WooCommerce compatibility.

🔌

Native WooCommerce Integration

Registered as a standard WooCommerce payment gateway — works seamlessly with both Classic and Block checkout.

🌍

15+ Global Providers

Connect PayPal, Stripe, Authorize.net, Adyen, Braintree, and many more — all through a single PaymentHood account.

⚡

Automatic Order Management

WooCommerce order statuses update automatically on payment success or failure. No manual order handling required.

🔒

Sandbox & Live Modes

Test your full checkout flow in sandbox mode before going live. Switch environments with a single toggle in the admin.

📦

Upload & Activate — That's It

Install the plugin from WordPress admin, activate it in WooCommerce settings, paste your API key, and you're ready to accept payments.

🎛️

Centralized Provider Dashboard

Manage provider credentials, view transactions, and configure settings from your PaymentHood account — not inside WordPress.
