Powerful Features for Modern Businesses

From fraud protection to real-time analytics, PaymentHood provides everything you need to optimize your payment operations and accelerate growth.

Unified Multi-Gateway Orchestration

Connect to 15+ payment providers through a single, consistent API. Switch between providers, add new ones, or route transactions intelligently—all without touching your code.

Advanced Fraud & Risk Management

Go beyond basic fraud detection with our customizable rules engine. Set granular controls for transaction velocity, geographic restrictions, and suspicious activity detection.

Real-Time Analytics & BI

Access comprehensive dashboards with live transaction monitoring, success rate analytics, provider performance metrics, and detailed financial reporting.

Secure Payment Links

Generate secure, one-time payment links for invoicing, or use our fully-customizable hosted checkout pages. Reduce PCI compliance burden while maintaining control.

Customer Lifecycle Management

Manage customer profiles, securely vault payment methods, handle complex subscription billing, and automate recurring payments with intelligent retry logic.

Enterprise Webhook System

Never miss critical payment events with our guaranteed webhook delivery system. Built-in retry logic, event sourcing, and detailed logging ensure perfect synchronization.

Multi-Tenancy & Team Management

Built for agencies and platforms managing multiple clients. Isolated app environments, granular role-based permissions, and comprehensive audit trails.

Account Verification & Risk Assessment

Integrate with Plaid for real-time bank account verification and balance checking before processing ACH payments, reducing NSF fees and improving success rates.

Intelligent Payment Routing

Automatically route transactions to the best-performing gateway based on success rates, cost optimization, currency support, and real-time provider health monitoring.
