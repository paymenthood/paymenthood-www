Flexible Transaction Control

Gain complete control over every step of your payment flow — from initial authorization to capture, refund, and dispute handling. PaymentHood unifies all transaction types in a single integration, giving you the flexibility to build any payment experience.

Sales

Process instant payments in a single step. Ideal for straightforward checkouts, subscriptions, or digital purchases where immediate capture is preferred.

Authorize & Capture

Reserve funds first, then capture when goods ship or services complete. Perfect for marketplaces, pre-orders, and on-demand platforms that require timing control.

Void Transactions

Cancel an authorization before capture with a single API call. Avoid unnecessary charges and improve the customer experience when an order is canceled early.

Refunds

Issue refunds directly from your dashboard or API. Keep customers happy while maintaining clear transaction tracking.

Dispute Management

Monitor, respond, and resolve chargebacks in one dashboard. Get real-time notifications, submit evidence easily, and track dispute outcomes across all payment providers.
