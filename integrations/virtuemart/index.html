How It Works

Up and Running in 5 Minutes

Three simple steps from download to accepting live payments on your VirtueMart store.

1
Download the Plugin

Grab the latest release from GitHub. It's a standard Joomla extension package — no build steps, no dependencies.

2
Install & Enable in Joomla

Go to Extensions → Manage → Install, upload the package, then enable the PaymentHood plugin — and it appears in your VirtueMart payment methods.

3
Connect Your PaymentHood Account

Paste your PaymentHood API key in the payment method settings, choose sandbox or live mode, and save. Your store is now ready to accept payments.
