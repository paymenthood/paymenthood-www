Frequently asked questions

What's included in the free plan?

Everything needed to accept payments: all gateways, all platform integrations, unlimited transactions, analytics, fraud protection, and full API access. Free forever, no credit card required.

Are there any transaction fees?

No. PaymentHood charges no transaction fees at any volume. You pay only what your payment gateway charges directly.

What does Checkout Pro add?

Full control over how your checkout page looks: your logo, your colors, your domain, your slogan, and no PaymentHood badge. Everything else stays the same.

Does the free plan show PaymentHood branding?

Yes. On the free plan your checkout page shows PaymentHood branding. Upgrading to Checkout Pro removes it completely and lets you apply your own design.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel your Checkout Pro subscription anytime and drop back to the free plan instantly.

How long does it take to set up?

For plugin integrations, setup takes under 5 minutes: download, upload, activate. For custom integrations, our team provides a dedicated migration guide and free support at no extra cost.

What happens if my transaction volume grows beyond $5,000/month?

The base plan is $29/month. For volume above $5,000/month, a 0.4% fee applies to the excess amount. Our pricing calculator above shows your exact cost at any volume. There are no surprise charges.

How long does it take to migrate from my current payment gateway?

For WHMCS and WooCommerce and all our integration plugins, setup takes under 5 minutes, download, upload, activate. For custom integrations, our team provides a dedicated migration guide and free support at no extra cost.
